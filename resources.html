Important Resources

Accessibility to information about deafness is an integral part in helping to understand this large community of people. It is one thing to simply know and live with the knowledge that deaf people exist, however, knowing about what issues they face that hearing people take for granted or have never even thought about could bring about a variety of ideas to make public spaces more deaf friendly. As such, here are some important resources. This can be helpful to both hearing and non-hearing individuals.

General Resources

Resources for Parents with a deaf child

Hands and Voices A group of parents and guradians of deaf or hard of hearing children sharing tips and learning from each other.

Baby Hearing : Similar to Hands and Voices, but it also offers profesional help and access to profesional resources.

American Society for Deaf Children : Offers different media for deaf and hard of hearing children and offers help for the next steps to come after being diagnosed.

Raising and Educating Deaf Children : Offers information and answers to common questions and concerns for raising a deaf child.
